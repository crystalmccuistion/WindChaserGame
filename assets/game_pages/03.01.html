I pray she doesn't give herself tetanus before I get up there—assuming she hasn't already.

"Stay right there!" I call up to her. "I'll come get you!"

"Hurry up then! If you really think you can make it!"

If I think I can—? I grit my teeth. That does it. I stand and bounce on the balls of my feet to loosen my joints, then set out for the base of the ruins.
