"Hey, Crystal!"

Startled, I lift my head and look around. I don't see the speaker anywhere.

"Up here!"

Shielding my eyes from the sun with one hand, I spot her: a tiny speck atop the starship ruins before me, waving its tiny arms. It's Cala, of course; she's the only other kid in our little band of travelers. Despite warnings about live wires, broken glass, and unfriendly wildlife, no kid I've known has been able to resist the allure of the abandoned starships for long.
